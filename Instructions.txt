Dragonslayer!

Fight your way through a seemingly endless cavern to purge the land of the evil dragon nest!

DIRECTIONS:

Movement is done using the arrow keys

The 'g' key picks up whatever item resides on your player's tile

The 'i' key displays your player's inventory with the option to use a selected item

The 'd' key displays your inventory with the option to drop a selected item

The 'c' key displays your character's information

The 's' key is used to go down stairs
